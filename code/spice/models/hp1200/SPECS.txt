
          	         GENERAL PROCESS SPECIFICATIONS FOR

			HP AMOSI 1.0 micron CMOS Bulk Wafers


HP AMOSI wafers use the HP CMOS34 specification (attached) with the
following additions for the linear capacitor option.

				Min	Nom	Max	Units
				---	---	---	-----
Nominal capacitance (C_0)	1.15	1.26	1.38	fF/um^2

Voltage coefficent (C_1)	1.04	1.51	2.07	aF/um^2/V

Voltage coefficent		0.09	0.12	0.15	%/Volt

Loss tangent @ 1 Mhz		0.14	0.17	0.20	-

Breakdown V for 150 aA/um^2	9.00	-	-	Volts

Leakage current	@ 5 V		-	-	95.0	aA/um^2

Dielectric absorption		-	-	1E-4	V/V

Self discharge rate		-	-	50.0	mV/sec


Capacitor oxide thickness	250	275	300	Angstroms




Definition of Terms

The capacitance is represented by a first order polynominal in voltage.
The designer must scale the coefficients to the area of the capacitor.

	C = (C_0 * A) + (C_1 * A * V)

Voltage cofficent = ( 1 / C ) * ( dC / dV ) = 100% ( C_1 / C_0 )

Loss tangent is the ratio of the real to the imaginary components of
the admittance phasor measured at 1 Mhz.

Self discharge rate = dV / dt = J_leakage / C_0
